Design Comp V1

I decided to choose the typefaces and the format of the project for the Design Comp V1. I didn't put a lot of elements on the webpage because I want to have an image as a piece of information for the reader, and then they read the text.

I also build a "read more" function for the reader, in case some of the readers don't like too much information. And at the end of the page, I design a section for the reader who wants to know how people prevent spreading fake news.
